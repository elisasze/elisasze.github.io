About Elisa

Elisa Sze is a metadata librarian at the University of Toronto Libraries (UTL). Prior to joining UTL, she was the collections and public services librarian at the University of Toronto's Faculty of Information (iSchool). She continues her connection with the iSchool as a sessional lecturer. Elisa has co-authored peer-reviewed articles on cataloguing, and her current research interests are in technical services management and Canadian library history.

Elisa holds a Master of Information Studies and Honours Bachelor of Arts (joint specialist in history and political science) from the University of Toronto, and an ARCT diploma in piano performance from the Royal Conservatory of Music (Toronto).

In her spare time, she likes making and thinking about paper and textile crafts.
